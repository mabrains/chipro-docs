A commit saves the selected changes in your project.

If this is your first commit:

![Empty](@site/static/img/chipro/designManager/versioning/VersionInit.png)

Click **Create First Commit** to initialize versioning.

---

## Commit Panel

The commit panel displays all detected changes, grouped by status:

* **Green – Added**
  ![Commit Panel Add](@site/static/img/chipro/designManager/versioning/CommitAdd.png)

* **Orange – Modified**
  ![Commit Panel Modify](@site/static/img/chipro/designManager/versioning/CommitModifiy.png)

* **Red – Deleted**
  ![Commit Panel Delete](@site/static/img/chipro/designManager/versioning/CommitDelete.png)

Enter a brief commit message, then click **Commit Changes** to save your updates.
