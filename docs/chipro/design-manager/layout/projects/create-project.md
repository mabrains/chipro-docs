Creating a project is the first step in organizing your design data.

---

## Opening the Project Form

Click **Create Project** or the **“+” button** in the Projects sidebar to open the form.

![New Project Dialog](@site/static/img/chipro/designManager/project/newProjectEmpty.png)

---

## Filling Project Details

Add your project information:

- **Project Name** (required)
- **Description** (optional)
- **PDK** (select a technology or leave unset)

Example:

![Project Filled](@site/static/img/chipro/designManager/project/newProject.png)

:::warning
**Once you have selected a PDK, you cannot change it later.**
:::

---

## Project Created

After submitting, the new project appears in the sidebar and becomes your active workspace.

![Project Created](@site/static/img/chipro/designManager/project/newProjectAdded.png)
