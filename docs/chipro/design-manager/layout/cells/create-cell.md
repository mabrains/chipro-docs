Creating a new cell is quick and follows a simple set of steps.

---

## 1. Open the Create Cell Dialog

Click the **“+” button** in the Cells panel.

![Create Cell Form Empty](@site/static/img/chipro/designManager/cell/CreateCellEmpty.png)

---

## 2. Enter the Cell Information

Fill in the fields:

- **Cell Name** — required
- **Description** — optional
- **Category** — optional (leaving it blank places the cell in _Uncategorized_)

If you choose a category, the cell will appear there immediately.

![Create Cell Form](@site/static/img/chipro/designManager/cell/CreateCell.png)

---

## 3. Create the Cell

Click **Create Cell**.

The new cell now appears in:

- **Everything**
- And its assigned category (if selected)

![Cell Created](@site/static/img/chipro/designManager/cell/CellCreated.png)
